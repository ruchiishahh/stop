Purpose

The entire purpose of our application is to ensure that the issues of domestic violence decreases and that more people are aware of the women who have suffered through it. In fact, our app name is an acronym that represents our goals and motives. The S stands for saving victims, the T stands for Teaching others about the victims, O stands for optimizing and taking advantage of technology to help the victims, and the P stands for providing resources and information to help the victims.
